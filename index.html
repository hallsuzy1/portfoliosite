About Me

Suzy Hall is an educator, instructional designer, and web developer. Her work focuses on equity and access to quality education for all students. Specifically she has focused her work on improving educational outcomes in underserved communities and addressing opportunity gaps throughout K-12 education.

With 10 years of experience in teaching, writing curriculum, and leading professional development, Suzy has been able to drive results to improve educational outcomes in communities throughout the United States.

Her areas of expertise include educational equity, diversity and inclusion, community building, social and emotional skill development, and positive behavior support.

Suzy is interested in the intersection of education and technology and believes that technology can improve educational outcomes for students and if implemented thoughtfully can be a strong force in reducing educational inequity.
